Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Compassion

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla. Headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, we operate across India addressing critical societal issues through a growing network of volunteers and corporate partners.

Our mission is to serve communities compassionately — and our vision is to ensure every underprivileged person grows with dignity and respect.

Section 8 Registered 80G & 12A Certified CSR-1 Registered NITI Aayog Registered IAF ISO 9001:2015
Our Initiatives

Flagship Projects

Each project targets a critical area of social need — from hunger and education to women's rights and the environment.

🍽️

Project SEVA

Providing food and clothing to the underprivileged. We have distributed over 50,000+ meals and essentials to homeless and destitute individuals across communities.

✦ 50,000+ beneficiaries served
📚

Project Bachpanshala

Ensuring quality education for underprivileged children by bridging gaps in digital literacy, life skills, and academic support for kids who lack access.

✦ Hundreds of children enrolled
🐾

Project Jeev

Protecting and caring for animals — from feeding stray animals to rescuing and rehabilitating wildlife. Because every life deserves care.

✦ Regular feeding drives ongoing
👩

Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development and financial independence programs. Helping women break barriers and build sustainable livelihoods.

✦ Women-led livelihood creation
🌿

Project Prakriti

Environmental conservation and sustainability efforts — from tree plantation drives to community awareness campaigns on protecting our natural resources.

✦ Multiple plantation drives
💼

Project Vikas

Enhancing employability through skill development and internship programs. Over 30,000+ interns trained across India in digital and professional skills.

✦ 30,000+ interns trained

Why It Matters

The Issues We Address

India's underprivileged communities face multiple crises at once. InAmigos tackles them together — because real change is holistic.

🎓

Lack of Education

Poverty pushes millions of children out of school. We step in with learning support and digital literacy.

🍱

Food Insecurity

Hunger remains a daily reality for many. Our feeding drives ensure no one sleeps on an empty stomach.

♀️

Women Disempowerment

Women deserve economic independence. We equip them with skills, mentoring, and opportunity.

🌳

Environmental Degradation

Deforestation and pollution threaten our future. We plant trees and build eco-awareness.

🐕

Animal Welfare

Strays and wildlife are vulnerable and voiceless. Our volunteers care for their feeding and safety.

📈

Youth Unemployment

Skills open doors. We train youth with real-world capabilities for a competitive job market.
